About Sound of Summer
Sound of Summer is a personal project exploring past summers I've experienced through the music I listened to. For over ten years I've been tagging songs in my iTunes library with the year and the season that I listened to them. I came up with the idea for this project while walking over a bridge shortly before I was attacked by a crow. Select songs are available to stream through Rdio.
About Me
My name is Chloe and I'm an emotional web developer living in Portland, Oregon. This is my first Rails adventure and oh my god it was so much fun. I have too many hobbies and a cat named FACE.

I'm vulnerable to rich emotional experiences in the summertime. The associated emotions get encoded into the music I listen to during that season.
